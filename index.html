About the PMP® Exam

The PMP® exam consists of 180 questions and must be completed within 240 minutes. Question types include scenario-based, multiple-response, drag-and-drop, and matching.

The exam is structured around three domains. People, Process, and Business Environment.

There are two optional 10-minute breaks. After a break, you cannot return to previous questions.
